What is Malware?

Malware (short for malicious software) is software created to damage, steal, or disrupt computers and networks. It comes in many forms, such as viruses, trojans, and worms. (Insert Link Here)

  • Viruses – spread through infected files
  • Trojans – pretend to be useful software
  • Worms – spread across networks automatically
